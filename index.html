Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Do I need a compliance background to use PRIAMTIV?+
Not at all. PRIAMTIV was designed for owners and operations leads — the people who already wear the compliance hat without the title. The questionnaires use plain language, and every recommendation tells you what to do next.
How is this different from a project tool or shared drive?+
A shared drive stores documents. A project tool tracks tasks. PRIAMTIV connects policies, risks, incidents, and assets so a change in one updates the others — and gives you an audit trail neither of those produces on its own.
What size businesses is PRIAMTIV built for?+
PRIAMTIV is built for small and growing businesses — typically 5 to 250 employees. The platform scales gracefully as you add departments, but the experience stays geared toward teams without a dedicated GRC function.
Is my data private to my company?+
Yes. PRIAMTIV is multi-tenant: every record carries your tenant ID and isolation is enforced at the database query level. No cross-tenant access by design, and private incidents are visible only to the reporter and admins.
Can I import my existing policies?+
Yes. Bring your existing policies in as drafts, version them inside PRIAMTIV, and publish when you're ready. The acknowledgement chain starts the moment you publish.
What if I just want to track incidents to start?+
That's a perfectly common starting point. Use the incident queue on its own — when you're ready, layer in policies, the risk assessment, or the asset register. Each module stands on its own and gets richer when combined.
