Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Do my employees need any technical background?

None at all. The courses are written for everyday staff — receptionists, owners, field crews — in plain language with practical, day-to-day examples.

How long does each course take?

Cyber Protection Awareness runs about 90 minutes; HIPAA & Data Protection Awareness about two hours. Both are self-paced, so learners can stop and resume anytime.

Do learners get a certificate?

Yes. Every learner who completes a course earns a dated certificate, and team administrators receive a completion report for their records.

Can you train my whole team?

Absolutely. Add your headcount in the enrollment form and we'll include team pricing and a simple way to track everyone's progress.

Is the HIPAA course enough for compliance?

It covers the workforce-training expectation for privacy and data protection awareness. We'll happily talk through your full obligations in a free consultation — and PRIAM can track policy acknowledgements alongside it.
